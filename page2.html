Gynaecology

Gynaecology is a medical specialty focused on the health of the female reproductive system, including the diagnosis and treatment of diseases related to it. It's often studied in conjunction with obstetrics, which focuses on pregnancy and childbirth, forming the combined field of obstetrics and gynaecology.

Gynaecologist

Gynecologists give reproductive and sexual health services that include pelvic exams, Pap tests, cancer screenings, and testing and treatment for vaginal infections. They diagnose and treat reproductive system disorders such as endometriosis, infertility, ovarian cysts, and pelvic pain.
