Orthopedics

Orthopedics, also known as orthopedic surgery, is a medical specialty focused on diagnosing and treating injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and tendons.

Orthopedist

The doctor who performs orthopedics is called an orthopedic surgeon or orthopedist. They specialize in diagnosing and treating conditions and injuries related to the musculoskeletal system, which includes bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, tendons, and nerves.
